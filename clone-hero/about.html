Clone Hero

Clone Hero is a free rhythm game, which can be played with any 5 or 6 button guitar controller, game controllers, or just your standard computer keyboard. Download the game, copy your song charts into the song folder and enjoy hours of fun!

I started playing this when I was like 8 or something and i loved it. We got an entire set, 2 guitars at first for guitar hero III then later we got a drumset, a microphone and a World Tour Guitar. All of This on the Wii. I was pretty proficient and I learned quickly, I started playing on easy than I leanred that and went on to the next difficulty. It didn't take long till I could pass songs on Expert.

And at some point I just stopped playing. Maybe because my skills weren't improving as much as before or because of the input lag of the Wii and the TV i was plaing on.

3 years ago i discoverd Clone Hero and it was a blessing. Nostalgia took over and I started to play on my keyboard like a guitar because I didn't know how to use my old guitar without the Raphnet Adapter. Later I discoverd you could use an old Wii-mote and some software to use a guitar. It was cumbersome and annoying to use but it worked. I played casually for a bit to try to and reignite my skills. I bit the bullet and bought the Adapter and it worked flawlessly. It was quite expensive but worth it. All of the annoying latency was gone and I was playing on 144hz monitor.

And now I'm here, I can get 96% - 99% on most expert songs. My songs library has grown to over 2500.
